Modal

On our modal we're expecting functionality that it opens on top of the page, moves keyboard (and screen reader) focus into it, and that the focus can't escape out.

Dialog

A dialog is similar functionally to a modal, except it doesnt have the ability to be closed by the user.

The user fundamentally needs to interact with content in the modal to be able to close it.

Interstitial

Interstitial modals are quite the different beast, they're opened and closed programmatically without user interaction. Usually after a fixed amount of time.
